**with:** Massimo Aprea and Michele Raitano

The Project aims to assess the impact of Reddito di Cittadinanza (Citizenship Income) on labor supply. By exploiting the administrative data on the beneficiaries, we apply standard matching and event-study techniques to estimate the causal effect of RdC on both extensive and intensive margins.
